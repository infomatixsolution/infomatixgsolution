Privacy Policy

At INFOMATIX GLOBAL SOLUTIONS, we are committed to safeguarding your personal data with the utmost transparency and integrity. Our Privacy Policy is designed to inform you how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in strict accordance with our business model, Google’s business practice requirements, and international data protection laws.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name, address, phone number, and email during registration or inquiries.
  • Financial Information: credit card or banking details when making payments.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, device type.
  • Service Data: service details (account numbers, support logs), collected via forms, cookies, or interactions.

Why We Collect Information

  • Provide Services: activate services, handle orders, and process account changes.
  • Customer Support: use contact and service data for support and troubleshooting.
  • Billing and Payments: process payments including execution fees ($49.99–$199.99).
  • Marketing: with consent, use non-identifying data to improve services and offer promotions.

Third-Party Service Providers

We collaborate with trusted vendors (e.g., AT&T, Verizon, Xfinity, Spectrum) and payment processors. While we enforce privacy compliance, their individual policies apply as well.

Transparency in Pricing and Billing

  • Execution Fees: range from $49.99–$199.99, displayed before commitment.
  • Separate Billing: charges from your provider (e.g., Spectrum, Verizon) are billed separately.
  • No Hidden Fees: all charges are clearly disclosed.

Data Security

  • Encryption: all sensitive data is encrypted.
  • Access Control: only authorized employees have access.
  • Secure Transactions: payments handled by trusted third parties.
  • Breach Notification: we notify users and authorities as required by law.

Your Rights

  • Request to view, update, or correct your personal information.
  • Request data deletion when no longer required.
  • Unsubscribe from marketing at any time.
  • Receive your data in a machine-readable format.

Compliance with Google Ad Policies

We strictly follow Google’s ad policies—no misleading pricing, affiliations, or deceptive practices. All services and costs are clearly communicated.

Policy Updates

This policy may be revised from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via our site or email.
